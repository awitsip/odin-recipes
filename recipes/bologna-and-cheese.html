Bologna and Cheese

A classic American Sandwhich

Generations after generations have been eating the good ol' Bologna and Cheese Sandwich

A quick and easy, and best part, very cheep option!

Ingredients

  • Bologna
  • Cheese
  • Bread
  • Hellmann's Mayonnaise

Steps

  1. Take 2 slices of bread
  2. Put mayo on both slices of bread
  3. Put a slice of Bologna on one slice of bread
  4. Put a slice of cheese on top of the Bologna
  5. Put the remaining slice of bread on top of the cheese
  6. Cut in a diagonal line to make triangles
  7. Enjoy!
