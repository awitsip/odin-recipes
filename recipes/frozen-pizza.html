Frozen Pizza

A step by step guide to make a Frozen Pizza

Who doesn't like a piping hot and freshly cooked pizza? Probably no one!

An easy and cheep go to for anyone needing a quick hot lunch or dinner, but don't forget there is now Breakfast Pizza!

Ingredients

  • 1 Frozen Pizza

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to temperature specified on the pizza's label
  2. Once oven is preheated place pizza on center rack in oven
  3. Cook until the edges are golden brown, checking frequently to avoid overcooking
  4. Remove from oven and let cool
  5. Cut into pie slices and cowabunga dude!
