Tomahawk Ribeye Steak

How to perfectly cook a Tomahawk Ribeye Steak

Going to a high class steak house can be an expensive outing, but if you like to grill, making your own steak house caliber ribeye at home can be a fraction of the price!

We will walk you through how to make this dream like scenario become reality!

Ingredients

  • 1 Tomahawk Ribeye Steak - ideally the largest you can find!
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Garlic Powder

Steps

  1. Take the steak ouf of the refrigerator well before you plan on cooking it, the longer the better for the steak to get as close to room temperature as possible before cooking
  2. Season generously with the Salt, Pepper and Garlic Powder
  3. Pre heat your grill for medium heat
  4. Cook the steak using indirect heat until an internal temperature of 115 degrees
  5. Once the steak hits the 115 internal temp set your grill to high heat
  6. Sear the steak directly over the heat source flipping every minute until the char is to your liking
  7. Remove the steak when it hits 135 degrees internally, you may need to finish cooking over indirect heat to get to 135 internal
  8. Let the steak rest for at least 5 minutes
  9. Slice against the grain and enjoy!
